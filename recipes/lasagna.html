Lasagna Recipe

Finished Lasagna

The lasagna contains several layers of thin and wide pasta noodles with marinated meat, cheese, and sauce.

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodle
  • Ground meat
  • Italian suace
  • Mozarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Season and cook meat in medium heat.
  2. Boil lasagna noodle.
  3. Place noodles in a container and apply a layer of meat with sauce and cheese.
  4. Repeat process to desired height.
