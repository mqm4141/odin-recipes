Mofongo Recipe

Mofongo

Mofongo is a fried and smashed preparation of the green platain.

Ingredients

  • Green Plantain
  • Garlic
  • Hot oil
  • Salt
  • Pork Rinds

Steps

  1. Heat the oil on high heat until temperature reacher 375 degrees F.
  2. Peel the green platain and cut it in one inch slices.
  3. Fry the plantain slices until fully cooked.
  4. Pour the cooked plantain slices into a Pilon.
  5. Add garlic, salt, and pork rinds for flavor and texture.
  6. Smash all ingredients until everything is mixed.
