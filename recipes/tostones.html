Tostones Recipe

Fried Tostones

Tostones is a fried variation of sliced plantain.

Ingredients

  • Green Plantain
  • Garlic
  • Hot oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Heat the oil on high heat until temperature reacher 375 degrees F.
  2. Peel the green platain and cut it in quarter inch slices.
  3. Fry the plantain slices until fully cooked.
  4. Add garlic and salt for flavor.
